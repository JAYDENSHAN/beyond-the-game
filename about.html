Our Mission

Youth sports should be about growth, teamwork, and fun. Our goal is to remove preventable injuries from the equation.

What We Do

At Beyond the Game, our mission is to empower and educate the youth in sports through the provision of reliable and easily understandable information about sports-related injuries, injury prevention methods, and recovery methods. We understand that injuries do more than just keep athletes from playing the sport they love; they can also disrupt youth development, leaving a serious, potentially lifelong impact on their overall wellbeing.


We believe that education can be among the most effective means to work towards our mission. Through various educational campaigns, outreach, and workshops we inform young athletes, their parents, coaches, and teams on how to identify various sports injuries, react to them properly, and how to avoid them in the future.
