Our Mission

Youth sports should be about growth, teamwork, and fun. Our goal is to remove preventable injuries from the equation.

Who We Are

Beyond the Game is a non-profit organization founded by former athletes and medical professionals. We saw too many young, promising athletes sidelined by injuries that could have been prevented with the right knowledge.


Today, our members travel to schools, clubs, and community centers to run interactive clinics. We teach proper body mechanics, recovery protocols, and the importance of overall wellness to keep the heart beating strong—just like our logo represents.
